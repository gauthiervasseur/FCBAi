# Overall Structure

* **AERIS → The umbrella initiative**
* **CAELIS → The research, leadership, and innovation center**
* **HORIZON → The venture studio building startups**

Think of the navigation logic like this:

```
AERIS (Umbrella Initiative)
│
├── CAELIS (Center / Research / Leadership / AI)
│
└── HORIZON (Venture Studio / Startup Creation)
```

The **AERIS page should inspire and frame the ecosystem**, while the other two pages **go deeper into execution**.

**CTA**

## 2. Why Horizon

### The Aerospace Startup Opportunity

A new wave of aerospace innovation is emerging:

* autonomous aerial systems
* next-generation satellite infrastructure
* AI-driven aviation
* climate technologies for aviation
* advanced propulsion
* defense and dual-use technologies

However, aerospace ventures face unique challenges:

* deep technical complexity
* long development cycles
* regulatory barriers
* high capital requirements

Horizon exists to help founders **navigate and overcome these challenges.**

---

### Venture Creation

Horizon helps build aerospace startups from early concepts to venture-scale companies.

We work with:

* founders

* industry experts

### Startup Acceleration

Horizon supports startups through:

* venture design
* technical mentorship
* go-to-market strategy
* investor introductions
* pilot partnerships with industry



### Venture Partnerships

We collaborate with:

* aerospace corporations
* venture capital firms
* government agencies
* research institutions

## 4. Venture Themes

This section helps investors understand the focus areas.

### AI for Aerospace

AI-enabled systems for flight, space, and aerospace operations.



### Autonomous Systems

Uncrewed aerial systems, autonomous navigation, and robotic aerospace platforms.



### Space Infrastructure

Technologies supporting the emerging space economy including satellites, data platforms, and orbital services.



### Sustainable Aviation

Technologies enabling lower-emission aviation and new energy systems.



### Defense & Dual-Use Technologies

Solutions with both commercial and defense applications.


## 5. How the Studio Works

**Step 1 — Opportunity Discovery**

Identify high-impact opportunities emerging from research, industry needs, and technological breakthroughs.



**Step 2 — Venture Formation**

Build founding teams and develop initial product and business models.


**Step 3 — Acceleration**

Support early-stage ventures through mentorship, partnerships, and investment connections.


**Step 4 — Scale**

Help companies raise capital and scale operations through the global aerospace ecosystem.
